Important Tech Milestones

Skyler Russell

In 1946 Eniac is created, the first "digital" programmable computer. A massive collection of circuit boards and wires. This computer was not made for people to buy as a personal computer and was instead used as a sort of calculator. It solved numerical problems.
In 1981 IBM's personal computer was the first released for personal usage.
In 1984 the apple macintosh was created and released. The first massively successful personal computer sold. The macintosh had a classic 80s look that a lot of people fell in love with. Both for design and for the style of the era. Even today some people still see the old backgrounds as something pleasing to look at.
In 1985 Windows is launched by microsoft. Windows 1.0 now existing.
In 1989 the world wide web is launched. Connecting people across the world.
In 1994 the sony playstation and the sega saturn is put into stores. Becoming some of the first popular gaming systems. With these two being released it made video games more normalized and widespread. More people seeing it's existence and getting into it as well.
In the 2000s, especially 2016 broadband internet is fixed and launched to help connect people faster than before.
